Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Jersey Jems

How far ahead should I order?

We recommend ordering 24-48 hours in advance, especially for specialty items and larger quantities. This ensures availability and gives us time to prepare your order properly.

Are products fresh or frozen?

All items are flash-frozen to preserve freshness during transport from our Northeast suppliers. They arrive frozen and ready to cook - just like how Italian families preserve their homemade pasta.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer several vegetarian options including our 5 Cheese Ravioli, Spinach & Cheese varieties, fresh pasta, and all our sauces (except those with meat).

Where do I pick up my order?

We have a convenient pickup location in Myrtle Beach. The exact address and detailed directions are provided with your order confirmation email.

Can I modify my order?

Yes! Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 12 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Contact us at info@jerseyjems.com or call (843) 555-JEMS.

How do I prepare the food?

Each order includes detailed reheating instructions. Most items can be prepared in 15-20 minutes directly from frozen - no thawing required!

Do you deliver?

Currently, we operate on a pickup-only model to keep costs low and ensure food quality. We're exploring delivery options for the future!

What are your prices?

Our prices range from $4.30 for fresh pasta to $25.60 for large family items. Check our complete menu for detailed pricing. Much more affordable than driving to NYC!
