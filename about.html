Our Story

From Northeast family kitchens to your Myrtle Beach table

The Jersey Jems Story

Born from frustration and fueled by passion for authentic Italian food.

After moving to Myrtle Beach from the Northeast, we quickly discovered what every transplant knows - you can't find decent Italian food down here. We're talking about real Italian food, not the watered-down versions that pass for "authentic" in tourist areas.

We missed the family-owned delis in Brooklyn, the legendary ravioli shops in South Jersey, and the corner markets in South Philly where grandmothers still hand-roll pasta. We missed food with soul, made by people who learned their craft from their parents and grandparents.

So we decided to bring it here ourselves.

Jersey Jems isn't just another food delivery service. We've partnered directly with the same family kitchens, legendary bakeries, and authentic Italian shops that have been serving the Northeast for generations. Every product we offer comes from real family recipes, made by people who've perfected their craft over decades.
