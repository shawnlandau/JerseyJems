Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering 24-48 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially for specialty items and larger quantities.

Are the products frozen or fresh?

All items are flash-frozen to preserve freshness during transport from Northeast suppliers. They arrive frozen and ready to cook.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer many vegetarian options including our 5 Cheese Ravioli, Spinach & Cheese varieties, all fresh pasta, and our homemade sauces (except those with meat). Look for the green "vegetarian" badge on menu items.

What are your pickup locations?

We have a convenient pickup location in Myrtle Beach. Exact address and directions provided with your order confirmation.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 12 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Contact us immediately for changes.

How do I reheat the products?

Each order comes with detailed reheating instructions. Most items can be prepared in 15-20 minutes from frozen.
